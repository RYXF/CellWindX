## Developers

**CellWindX** was developed and maintained by **Xiaofeng Yang**, a graduate student, and **Dr. Shan Li**, a postdoctoral fellow, from the **Deng Liang Research Group at Chongqing Medical University**.

**Dr. Shan Li**, M.D., Ph.D., is a resident physician and postdoctoral fellow in the **Department of Gastroenterology, The First Affiliated Hospital of Chongqing Medical University**. He has participated in the National Science and Technology Major Project, three projects funded by the National Natural Science Foundation of China, and two projects funded by the Chongqing Natural Science Foundation. His long-term research focuses on the basic mechanisms of liver diseases and liver cancer, as well as clinical research in gastrointestinal endoscopy. He has published 11 SCI-indexed articles as first author or corresponding author in journals including *Clinical and Molecular Hepatology*, *Gastrointestinal Endoscopy*, *OncoImmunology*, and *Digestive and Liver Disease*, as well as one article indexed by CSCD.
